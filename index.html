tic-tac-toe

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe game!

Choose from a variety of gameboards: from the classic 3x3 to the larger 6x6.

The starting player is chosen randomly. Then, each one marks an empty cell in turn.

On a 3x3, be the first to align 3 marks to win. On larger boards, aim for 4.

Form multiple lines to get more points!

1 Player. Test your skills against the AI – from Novice to Expert level, everyone can fit.

2 Players. Challenge a friend and see who outsmart the other.

Extended Mode. With limited marks available, each successive new mark replaces the oldest one. Remember past moves to prevail!

Ready to make your move? Let the game begin!
